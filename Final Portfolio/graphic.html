Broken & Caged.
In today's society, people have high demands on their appearance and body shape, pursuing a slim waist, less than 45 kg weight, and delicate makeup. Under the influence of society, "thinness" and "beauty" have become a prison for people's aesthetics, making people in pursuit of beauty fall into an endless abyss, and they rely on drugs to make their weight drop. "Thinness" and "beauty" constantly affect people's thinking, hurting their bodies like thorny tentacles and broken glass.

02.

Prohibition Definition.
In collage, I used magazines as my cutting material. At first, I tear out the character and color sections attached to my eyes. And then I college the page together with the choice parts I need. Moreover, I use scissors or hand-tears to cut out the pieces I want to put into the collage. Finally, I put the cut-out part on the paper, arranging and overlapping them to try to achieve the desired effect. I think the fashion magazines have more of the color and character parts that I need, so I use more of the content from fashion magazines in my collages. After college, I wanted to make my work show in a visual way, so I combined hand-drawn and blurred filter effects in the painting to increase the visual effect of the work.
